Optimizing Shipping for a Sustainable Future

Why BlueVect?

  • The shipping industry carries out more than 90% of global trade and is responsible for about 3% of global CO2 emissions (IMO, 2020).
  • By 2050, emissions from the shipping industry could increase by up to 130% compared to 2008 levels.
  • However, Green House Gas (GHG) emissions from the shipping industry in 2050 should be half of the emission level in 2008 (Mepc, 2018).
  • Optimizing ship operation behavior to minimize fuel consumption can play a significant role in achieving these targets.
  • Accurate and reliable weather forecasting data is key to enabling this optimization (R. Zaccone, Ottaviani, Figari, and Altosole, 2018).

Our Algorithm

  • BlueVect uses real-time data on wind patterns and ocean currents to calculate the most energy-efficient routes for ships.
  • By leveraging these natural forces, BlueVect helps reduce fuel consumption and minimize environmental impact.
  • Advanced algorithms ensure that shipping companies can achieve their sustainability goals while optimizing operational efficiency.
